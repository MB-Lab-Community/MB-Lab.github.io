About MB-Lab

MB-Lab is an open source project based off of the famous ManuelBastioniLAB.
Manuel Bastioni had started to develop this addon for Blender in 2016 and over the next two years was very successful in updating this character creation tool. During the fall of 2018 he decided to stop developing this addon for reasons that are his own. Since the project was developed with an open source license it was taken upon the community to continue developing this amazing tool for the next generation of Blender.
The project is hosted on GitHub and is maintained by an active community.
